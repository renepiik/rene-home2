Projects

  • The Estonian language in mathematical literature Fall 2021
    A report on how Estonian vocaburaly has changed in mathematical texts over the 150 years they have been in print. I observe when the biggest changes in mathematical language took place and who were the driving figures behind modernizing it.
  • UniTartu Progress 2020-2021
    Twitter bot for tracking the progress of the ongoing semester at the University of Tartu
  • Personal Library Spring 2020
    A Java app for managing your personal library. Accessible by command line and by using a GUI.
  • Lazare Carnot's biography and proof of Carnot's theorem Spring 2018
    A paper on the French politician, engineer and mathematician Lazare Carnot and an analytic proof of Carnot's theorem. The first half of the paper explores his exciting life, his role in French politics and contributions to Napoleon's war efforts. The second half proposes an alternative analytic proof to Carnot's theorem.
